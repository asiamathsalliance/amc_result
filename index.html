Founded in 1950, the AMC is the preeminent math competition for students K-12. Today, over 300,000 students in 50 states and over 30 countries take the AMC to bolster their confidence and passion for math.

AMC 10/12 2025

The AMC 10/12 is a 25-question, 75-minute multiple-choice competition designed for students in Grade 10/12 and below. It covers mathematical topics such as elementary algebra, basic geometry, elementary number theory, and introductory probability.

AMC 8 2026

The AMC 8 is a 25-question, 40-minute math contest for students in grade 8 and below, focusing on middle school topics like counting, probability, geometry, proportions, graphs, and basic algebra.
