American Mathematics Competitions

Founded in 1950, the AMC is the preeminent math competition for students K-12. Today, over 300,000 students in 50 states and over 30 countries take the AMC to bolster their confidence and passion for math. Please note that only students within Southeast Asia and those attending schools affiliated with the Asia Math Alliance have their scores reported here.

About Us

Asia Math Alliance

We support students and schools through high-quality mathematics competitions and enrichment pathways. Our mission is to build confidence, curiosity, and problem-solving excellence by connecting learners across Southeast Asia with meaningful contest experiences.

HIGH SCHOOL

AMC 10/12 2025

The AMC 10/12 is a 25-question, 75-minute multiple-choice competition designed for students in Grade 10/12 and below. It covers mathematical topics such as elementary algebra, basic geometry, elementary number theory, and introductory probability.

Middle School

AMC 8 2026

The AMC 8 is a 25-question, 40-minute math contest for students in grade 8 and below, focusing on middle school topics like counting, probability, geometry, proportions, graphs, and basic algebra.

Please see below to access your results in the AMC 8 2026.

Invitational

AIME II 2026

The American Invitational Mathematics Examination (AIME) is a 15-question, 3-hour math contest for top scorers in the AMC 10/12. It is an advanced competition that requires creative problem-solving in algebra, geometry, number theory, and combinatorics, and only students who qualify through the AMC 10 or 12 are invited to participate.
